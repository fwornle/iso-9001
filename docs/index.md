    ## How to Use This Documentation

    ### :one: For Audit Preparation

    1. **Review the [Audit Catalogue](getting-started/audit-catalogue.md)** — Familiarize yourself with typical questions for your area
    2. **Fill in Reference Documents** — Replace placeholders with your unit-specific information
    3. **Follow the [Preparation Checklist](getting-started/preparation-checklist.md)** — Track progress through phased timeline
    4. **Gather Evidence** — Collect actual records demonstrating process execution

    ### :two: Before the Audit

    !!! warning "Final Readiness Check"
        Complete the day-of checklist in the [Preparation Checklist](getting-started/preparation-checklist.md)

    - Ensure all documents are reviewed and approved
    - Organize evidence folders for easy access
    - Conduct mock interviews with team members
    - Brief team on audit expectations

    ### :three: During the Audit

    !!! info "Speaking to Auditors"
        Use suggested answers as guidance — speak naturally, not scripted. Auditors want to see that:

        - ✓ Processes exist and are documented
        - ✓ Processes are followed consistently
        - ✓ Results are measured
        - ✓ Improvements are made based on data

    Have reference documents ready but lead with your actual practice. Point to real examples and evidence.
