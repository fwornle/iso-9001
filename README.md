## How to Use

### Step 1: Read the Catalogue
Start with [ISO-9001-Audit-Catalogue.md](ISO-9001-Audit-Catalogue.md) — it contains **70+ typical auditor questions** organized by ISO 9001 clause, with suggested answers tailored to our DDD unit context.

### Step 2: Fill in the Reference Documents
Each REF document is a **template with structure and examples**. Fill in your unit-specific details:
- Replace `_[Name]_`, `YYYY-MM-DD`, and `_[Fill in]_` placeholders
- Add your actual risks, stakeholders, objectives, and metrics
- Get documents reviewed and approved by the designated owners

### Step 3: Follow the Preparation Checklist
[Audit-Preparation-Checklist.md](Audit-Preparation-Checklist.md) provides a phased timeline:
- **Phase 1** (8-12 weeks out): Complete documentation
- **Phase 2** (6-8 weeks out): Gather evidence
- **Phase 3** (4-6 weeks out): Train people, conduct mock interviews
- **Phase 4** (1-2 weeks out): Final readiness checks

### Step 4: Study the Process Map
[QMS-Process-Map.md](QMS-Process-Map.md) shows how all processes interact — auditors love seeing this.

## Key Actions Needed

1. **Appoint a QMR** (Quality Management Representative) if not already done
2. **Run at least one management review** before the audit (use REF-12 template)
3. **Complete at least one internal audit cycle** (use REF-11 program)
4. **Have 3-5 CAPA records** showing complete corrective action cycles
5. **Populate KPI dashboards** with at least 2-3 quarters of trend data
6. **Conduct mock audit interviews** with all spokespersons
